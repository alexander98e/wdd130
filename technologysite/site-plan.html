Content

Home page

The plan for the main page is that the user or visitor has the opportunity to find an overview of all the categories that could be of benefit to him, such as new news about technological advances (page2) depending on the category in the technological area a hyperlink will connect you and direct you to pages with new posts about what you have chosen, so you will also have the option to connect with verified scientific channels on YouTube. Also on the main page you will have the option to go to a forum where users can share or post links (page3), on this page users can interact with the community of science lovers. So also our main page should be organized so that users can navigate and be guided with it very easily, all this thanks to the texts, images and icons. Almost in the middle of our main page, a legend will be inserted that will show our vision and mission about the objectives of our page, which will reflect our "personality as a page". Already in the footer we will publish our social networks such as facebook, youtube and instagram. Answering this question: "Why will your visitors come to your site instead of another to get this information or complete the actions?" : The purpose of our website is to be able to provide the tools for our community to be informed without the risk of being deceived, wanting to support with correct intentions, always walking in the margin of our values ​​as human beings.
